# Geo Owl Information Environment Analyst — Independent Candidate Campaign

This public repository presents Russell Dudek's candidate vision for Geo Owl's Information Environment Analyst role supporting the U.S. Space Force Joint Commercial Operations cell.

## Thesis
**Map the delta before it becomes the decision.**

The campaign proposes a Narrative Delta Protocol for comparing localized public-information conditions against a baseline, corroborating meaningful change, issuing a decision cue, and learning through Rapid Recaps and After-Action Reviews.
